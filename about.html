Who We Are

Your Reliable Partner in Protective Wear

Global Safety Wear (Pty) Ltd was founded with a clear mission: to provide businesses, healthcare professionals, and industrial workers across South Africa with reliable access to high-quality nitrile gloves at competitive prices.

Based in Ekandustria, Bronkhorstspruit, we operate as a manufacturer and supplier, giving us the ability to offer consistent quality and stable supply — even during periods of high demand. We understand that for many of our customers, gloves are not optional; they are critical to safety, hygiene, and compliance.

We work directly with healthcare facilities, laboratories, food manufacturers, salons, and industrial companies, providing tailored solutions that meet the specific volume and quality requirements of each sector we serve.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to quality, safety, and customer service.

🏆

Uncompromising Quality

We supply only nitrile gloves that meet strict quality standards. Our products are tested for durability, tensile strength, and barrier protection — because your safety cannot be compromised.

🤝

Customer-First Approach

We treat every customer as a long-term partner. Whether you're placing a small order or a large-volume bulk purchase, you'll receive the same attentive, personalised service.

🚚

Reliable Supply & Delivery

We understand that running out of gloves can halt operations. That's why we maintain steady stock and deliver across South Africa within 5–7 working days, with free delivery in Gauteng.

🌱

South African Pride

We are a proudly South African business, rooted in our community and committed to growing local supply chains. Buying from us supports local enterprise and job creation.

🔬

Medical-Grade Standards

Our gloves are suitable for medical, dental, laboratory and food-safe applications. We stock powder-free options that meet hygiene and allergen requirements.

💬

Transparent Communication

We keep customers informed throughout the order and delivery process. No hidden fees, no surprises — just honest business practice every step of the way.
